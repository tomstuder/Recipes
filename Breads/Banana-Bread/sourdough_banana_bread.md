# Sourdough Banana Bread Recipe

## Prep Time:
- 15 minutes

## Cook Time:
- 45 mins - 1 hour

## Servings: 
- 10-12 slices

## EQUIPMENT
- (2) 8x4" loaf pans
- 3 mixing bowls
- Measuring cups and spoons
- Whisk
- Spatula
- Hand or stand mixer (optional)
- Wire cooling rack

## INGREDIENTS

**Wet Ingredients**
- 8 tablespoons (113 g) unsalted butter
- 1 cup (200 g) brown sugar
- 2 large eggs
- 3 medium ripe bananas
- 3 tablespoons (45 g) greek yogurt
- 1 teaspoon (5 g) vanilla extract
- 1/2 cup (125 g) sourdough starter (discard or active)

**Dry Ingredients**
- 2 cups (240 g) flour (all-purpose or bread flour)
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon (5 g) fine sea salt

## Add-ins
*If using add-ins, keep the total amount to ½–¾ cup.*
- Milk chocolate chips
- Pecans or walnuts

## INSTRUCTIONS
1. Preheat oven to 350°F (176°C). Grease two 8x4" loaf pans and set aside.
2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, salt, baking powder, and baking soda. Set aside.
3. In a separate bowl, mash the bananas until smooth. Set aside.
4. In a third bowl, soften the butter in the microwave in 15-second intervals.
5. Add the brown sugar, eggs, sourdough starter, Greek yogurt, vanilla extract, and mashed 
   bananas to the melted butter. Stir until smooth.
6. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and stir until just combined — do not overmix. 
   If using add-ins, gently fold them in with a spatula.
7. Divide the batter evenly between the two loaf pans.
8. Bake for 45–60 minutes, starting to check at 45 minutes. Insert a toothpick into the center 
   — the loaves are done when it comes out clean.
9. Cool in the pans for 15 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.

## Notes
- Store at room temperature for up to 3 days, wrapped in plastic wrap or in an airtight container.
- Store in the freezer for up to 3 months, wrapped tightly in plastic wrap then foil.
