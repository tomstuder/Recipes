# Sourdough Banana Bread Recipe

## Prep Time:
- 15 minutes

## Cook Time:
- 1 hour

## Servings: 
- 10-12 slices

## EQUIPMENT
- 9" x 5" loaf pan
- Mixing Bowl
- Measuring cups/spoons
- Spatula
-  Mixer

## INGREDIENTS

**Wet Ingredients**
- 8 tablespoons (113 g) unsalted butter
- 1 cup (200 g) brown sugar
- 2 large eggs
- 3 medium ripe bananas
- 3 tablespoons (45 g) greek yogurt
- 1 teaspoon (5 g) vanilla extract
- 1/2 cup (125 g) sourdough starter (discard or active)

**Dry Ingredients**
- 2 cups (240 g) flour (all-purpose or bread flour)
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon (5 g) fine sea salt

## Add-ins
*Be mindful of the additions total inclusions to be 1/2 - 3/4 cup in total*
- 1/2 to 3/4 cup milk chocolate chips
- 1/2 cup pecans or walnuts

## INSTRUCTIONS
1. Preheat oven to 350°F (176°C) and lightly grease a 9" x 5" loaf pan.
2. Whisk the flour, salt, baking powder, and baking soda together in a bowl and set aside. 
3. In a separate bowl mash the bananas with a fork until smooth and set aside.
4. In a third bowl, melt the butter in the microwave. To the melted butter add the brown sugar, eggs, sourdough starter discard, vanilla extract, greek yogurt, and mashed bananas and stir them with a spatula until the mixture is smooth.
5. Add the wet and dry ingredients together and stir until just combined. (Do not over mix!) If adding walnuts, gently fold them into the batter with a spatula.
6. Pour the batter into the loaf pan and bake for 50-60 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
7. Allow the banana bread to cool in the loaf pan for 15 minutes. Next, gently run a knife along the inside of the pan to help release the loaf. Carefully transfer it onto a wire rack. Let the loaf rest on the cooling rack until it cools down, making it easier to handle and allowing it to set slightly before you slice it.

## Notes
- Store at room temperature for up to 3 days, wrapped in plastic wrap or in a container with a lid.
- Store in freezer for up to 3 months tightly wrapped in foil and plastic.
